Travel in Style

The Netherlands

Known for its windmills, tulips, and canals, the Netherlands is a beautiful country to explore. Don't miss out on the famous Keukenhof Gardens, and visit the capital city, Amsterdam, for its rich cultural history and vibrant atmosphere.

Food to Try:

  • Poffertjes - Small, fluffy pancakes typically served with powdered sugar.
  • Stroopwafels - A delicious caramel-filled waffle cookie.
  • Haring - Raw herring fish, typically served with onions and pickles.

Places to Visit:

  • Keukenhof Gardens - Famous for its stunning tulips and seasonal flowers.
  • Rijksmuseum - A museum housing Dutch art, including works by Rembrandt.
  • Canal Cruise in Amsterdam - Explore the city from the water.

Paris, France

Paris, the city of love, is a must-see destination. From the Eiffel Tower to the Louvre, you’ll find iconic landmarks and delicious pastries to indulge in. Explore Montmartre for its charming streets and local artists.

Food to Try:

  • Croissants - Buttery, flaky pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Escargots - Snails cooked in garlic butter for a daring French dish.
  • Crêpes - Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory fillings.

Places to Visit:

  • Eiffel Tower - An iconic landmark and a must-see for anyone visiting Paris.
  • Louvre Museum - Home to famous works of art, including the Mona Lisa.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral - A Gothic masterpiece and one of the most famous churches in the world.

Germany

Rich in history, Germany offers everything from medieval castles to vibrant modern cities. Explore Berlin’s wall history, visit Neuschwanstein Castle, and don’t forget to try a delicious bratwurst!

Food to Try:

  • Bratwurst - Traditional German sausage, often grilled or pan-fried.
  • Sauerkraut - Fermented cabbage, often served with meats.
  • German Pretzels - Soft, salty bread snacks.

Places to Visit:

  • Brandenburg Gate - A symbol of Germany’s reunification.
  • Neuschwanstein Castle - A fairy-tale castle located in the Bavarian Alps.
  • The Black Forest - A region of scenic beauty, known for its forests and quaint villages.

Italy

Italy is famous for its stunning art, history, and, of course, its cuisine. Whether you’re exploring the ruins of Rome or enjoying a gondola ride in Venice, Italy offers a rich cultural experience at every corner.

Food to Try:

  • Pasta Carbonara - A Roman specialty made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.
  • Pizza Margherita - A classic pizza topped with tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.
  • Tiramisu - A coffee-flavored Italian dessert made of layers of sponge cake soaked in coffee.

Places to Visit:

  • Colosseum - Ancient Roman gladiatorial arena in the heart of Rome.
  • Venice Canals - Explore Venice’s romantic canals by gondola.
  • Leaning Tower of Pisa - The famous tilted tower in Pisa.
